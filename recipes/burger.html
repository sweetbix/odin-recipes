Burger

Description

A delicious burger, very easy to make, super healthy and yummy!

Following recipe is very simple, anyone can follow along, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Beef patty
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook beef patty
  2. Set up buns with lettuce and tomato
  3. Whilst patty is still cooking, lay a slice of cheese to melt on top of it
  4. When patty is well done, move it all onto buns
  5. Finish off with tomato sauce
  6. Enjoy!
